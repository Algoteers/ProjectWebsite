The Startup

Advanced Algos Ltd.

Advanced Algos Ltd. is the startup developing the AAPlatform –a system designed to empower developers wishing to work on their own trading bots—by hosting specialized cloud infrastructure, web tools and fully-functional open source algobots to serve as templates and a vantage point to get devs up and running in less than a minute.

The Autonomous Agents

Algobots

They are open source algorithms running on the AAPlatform and designed to consume each other's services as autonomous agents with the ultimate goal of automating crypto trading strategies by harnessing collective algobot intelligence. Bots are powered by ALGO, so we call them algobots.

The Algobot Makers

Algobots Community

The Algobots Community is an incipient crowd of developers and traders joining forces in Algobot Teams to create state-of-the-art algobots that compete with each other in open trading competitions —one of the key drivers for algobots evolution.

The Energy

ALGO

ALGO is the energy that powers de grid of resources of the ALGO Ecosystem. It is the platform's native token that functions as a medium of exchange within the network.
