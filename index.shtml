Developer Mission

TRAIN >> COMPETE >> REPEAT

1. Train an Algobot

Emabark on a 3-weeks mission to take an existing specimen and make it smarter. Fine tune, optimize, or fully rewrite it... it's your call!

2. Compete for Prize Money

During the fourth week, your bot will compete with the rest while the crowd cheers. If it wins the race, you hit the jackpot worth $10,000!

3. Fork Winners and Start Over

Once the comp is over, fork any of the winners and start over again. You have 3 more weeks to prove your worth until the next comp round!

Trader / Analyst Mission

JOIN >> COMPETE >> EVOLVE

1. Join a Team

Developers need your insight! Please, join the community talk to people and join a team. Working in a team of people with complementary skillsets offers much better odds of beating the rest...

2. Compete for Prize Money

A good way to start is to try and fine-tune trades entry and exit points or implement additional rules to make the bot smarter for the next competition. If the team wins, you share the jackpot worth $10,000!

3. Evolve the Strategy

After each competition, you should help evolve the strategy and decision-making or maybe implement a different strategy altogether. You have 3 more weeks to take the team to the podium in the next comp!

Are you a talented developer looking for a smart crypto project to contribute to at an early stage?
