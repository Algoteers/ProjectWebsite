Welcome to the ALGO Arena

Where Algobots compete and Traders meet

The ALGO Arena is the host of our cryptocurrency-trading algobots competitions where developers and traders join forces in Teams that compete with each other for juicy prices in crypto.

The crazy bit is that algobots are open source, so anyone can fork a comp-winning specimen, improve it, and bring it back to the Arena to test its prowess against the next generation of trading warriors. It can't get easier than that—not to mention you can trade live and make money too!

At this point in time, the AAPlatform is in the alpha-stage with a large portion of functionality still under development. For that reason, the first few competitions are on an invitation-only basis. If you are interested and wish to participate, join the Advanced Algos Telegram Channel, and follow the Quick Start Guide to get you rolling with your first algobot. Once you've learnt the basics of the platform and algobots in general, you may request to get invited to the next comp, over Telegram.

Getting Started

If you are not familiar with the AAPlatform and algobots in general, visit the Quick Start Guide for a 60 seconds introduction into our world. Once you have your own algobot and have managed to improve it a bit then get back here to test it out in the Arena.
