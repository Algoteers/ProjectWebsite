Evolving Collective Trading Intelligence

Advanced Algos is a technology project featuring a platform that enables the creation of open source trading bots participating in trading competitions. The community forks winners, improves them and brings them back to the arena for the next round, kick starting a relentless evolutionary process.

Use our IDE to develop algobots on the browser

  • Debug visually on the charts and also in the code.

  • Change algobots code with the browser's developer tools.

  • No arbitrary barriers imposed on your creativity. You may code anything JavaScript allows you to.

Start with a fork of the last competition winner algobot

  • Algobot optimization and efficiency developed by the combined force of community collaboration and the constant iterative improvements gained through ongoing cycles of competition.

  • Public access and verification of algobot evolutionary track record.

Take part in trading comps and earn prizes in crypto

  • Take your bot to the Arena and test it's prowess against other algobots.

  • Playing field is leveled after each round so that no one is left behind.

  • Found a Team and get help from friends and coleagues.

Opportunity for Developers

Advanced Algos is a project with a revolutionary approach to the field of crypto-trading.

In order to produce profitable trading algorithms starting from scratch, we enable and put in motion certain dynamics that are expected to produce an environment in which our trading algorithms evolve at much faster rates than those of potential competitors. This means that, even if we start with pretty stupid specimens, our bots should outperform all other trading bots sooner or later.

The project and the technology around it is currently under development. If you are a talented developer willing to participate in an open source crypto-project in exchange for project tokens, we are happy to invite you to join the community on Telegram and introduce yourself.
