The Advanced Algos Project is shaped around a vision in which crypto trading is no longer the domain of an elite

Trading bots outperform humans in crypto trading just like a strong chess engine beats the chess world champion

The abilities to perform calculations crunch data and analyze complex situations in seconds give algorithms a clear edge over humans. Moreover, algorithms work 24/7/365 and leave the emotional factor out of the equation. It is indisputable that –in the near future—virtually all profitable crypto trading will be done by bots.


Closed source bots hit traditional markets decades ago, owned –mostly—by companies that protect their intellectual property and keep profits for themselves. However, there is only so much private corporations can do: their limited resources and their inability to attract the best talents are a critical hindrance.


Successful open source projects develop worldwide communities and –many times—are able to attract the best minds on earth. Permissionless innovation dynamics have proven to be a powerful catalyst for the development of many industries and trading bots will be no exception.


Now, let’s do some guessing... Who do you think is best suited to conquer the most market share in the crypto trading space?

  • A corporation with a number of programmers and scientists on the payroll working eight-hours shifts?

  • A crypto startup with a closed source platform totally missing the point of permissionless innovation and the new sharing economy?

  • A worldwide community of developers, traders, analysts, scientists, entrepreneurs and crypto holders...

    • working together towards a common goal,

    • collaborating by polishing and improving each other's algobots,

    • competing to conquer top spots on trading competitions with substantial monetary prizes,

    • fine-tuning and tweaking winning algobots in a never-ending evolutionary process,

    • building reputation and a public track record for their algobots to gain crypto holders' subscriptions

    ... all within an ecosystem in which open source and permissionless innovation constitute key drivers for rapid evolution?


Fork ⇝ Tune ⇝ Compete ⇝ Evolve

The Project's Mission

  • Develop a platform for easy algobots' creation and operation.
  • Bootstrap and incentivize a multi-talent community developing algobots.
  • Provide a sustainable business model catering to all crypto holders.
  • Encourage permissionless innovation and gamified competition to drive algobots’ rapid evolution.
